Who Am I

I`m Tamim Ashour, 34, wells completions engineer, and business entrepreneur . I was exited to join this camp, in order to build and develop coding skills that will directly add enhancement and improvement to startups I`m involved in, also looking forward to gain the access into more advanced fields in the industry.

2008 graduated from Jordan University with a degree in Applied Geology, and I got the chance to work in different projects, mainly in the oil field, and I was lucky to work with global company SHELL for 6 years, during that period I managed to add another degree to my portfolio from Shell university in Netherlands, in Oil Wells Completion engineering & design. For me working in Oil field was fun and tough at the same time, during that time I recognized my tendency toward technology specially softwares, I used to read about it everytime I had a chance, I learned some Python, by myself.

Later on I had some attempts to jump inside some startups in the technology, only to keep updated, till I had my own attempts in the field, and usually I used to use other developers in order to achieve the project`s goals. Now I`m involved in two early stages startups one is a virtual education platform called 3abkari.com, the other one is smart device including electronics and softwares.
